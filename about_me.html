About Me

Hi, I’m Arshia Faisal, an 18-year-old student and aspiring creative developer and designer. I enjoy working at the intersection of design and code, where I can turn ideas into clean, functional and visually pleasing digital experiences.

I am currently studying and building my technical foundation, while also exploring different tools, languages, and design styles. Alongside my studies, I’ve taken on small projects and opportunities that helped me understand how real-world work feels, from meeting deadlines to collaborating and presenting my ideas.

Over time, I’ve developed skills in web development, AI design, and creative problem-solving. I like structuring pages so they feel simple and easy to use, and I pay a lot of attention to alignment, spacing, and typography. I also enjoy experimenting with layouts, color palettes, and small interactive details that make a website feel more personal.

My work experience includes student projects, workshops, and freelance-style tasks where I’ve created designs, coded front-end layouts, and learned how to adapt to different styles and requirements. Even though I’m still early in my journey, I’ve been practicing and learning for the past few years and I’m constantly trying to improve with every new project I take on.

In the future, I hope to keep growing as a developer and designer, build a strong portfolio of work, and collaborate with people who value creativity, clarity, and thoughtful digital experiences.
